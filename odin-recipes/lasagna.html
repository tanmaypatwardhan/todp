Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving

Lasagna layering order

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with remaining parmesan
